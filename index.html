The site for managing household devices in a smart home. This is an introductory task of the School of Interface Development (the Yandex curriculum, the analogue of the Rolling Scopes school). According to requirements of the task I have developed a site based on the proposed layout. In my project, device information (device name, device status, device type) is stored in a json file.
